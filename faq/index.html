    FAQ

    Q: How do I get the links after the bot's post to not show up?

    A: Sadly the only way to do this is to manually edit the permissions for each channel. To open the permissions for a channel click on the cog next to the channel name

    Next, on the Permissions tab, click on the bot's role (it should just be the name of the bot). If you don't see it you will have to click the plus and it should appear in the dropdown.

    Any squares that have a "/" inside of them mean that they inherit their value from the server-wide permissions (at least in theory...this doesn't seem to work all that well) the only thing you need to change ist to click on the box next to "Embed links" untill it is a red "X".
